The Thesis

Why This Exists

Attention already exists around athletes and founders in overlooked markets. The problem is that decision-makers — sponsors, investors, city planners — can’t act on attention alone. They need verifiable stories: real performance data, clear contract thinking, and honest project context. Without that, deals stall or never start.

TMM reduces discovery and due-diligence friction by packaging those stories into watchable content that decision-makers can share, reference, and act on.

For Brands

See on camera who can credibly deliver on a campaign — before committing budget or legal overhead.

For Investors & Partners

Understand how an athlete or founder actually thinks about contracts, risk, and execution — not just highlights.

For Cities & Operators

Get story assets built around real project data that hold up when asking for sponsorship, grants, or capital.
